｡˚ ❀Juan Crisostomo Ibarra y Magsalin༘⋆｡

 Known as 'Ibarra' in the story, a wealthy young man and the son of Don Rafael Ibarra. His father sent him to Europe to study for 7 years until he came back to the Philippines.
